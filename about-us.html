About ElectroTech

Changing the way Indonesia is powered

History

ElectroTech Indonesia was founded in 2004 with a vision to revolutionize the electrical services industry in Indonesia. What began as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a leading provider of comprehensive electrical solutions. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has driven our growth and success over the years.

Our milestones over the years:

  • 2004: ElectroTech Indonesia was established with a focus on electrical system design and installation.
  • 2008: Expanded services to include maintenance and troubleshooting, ensuring long-term reliability for our clients' electrical systems.
  • 2012: Launched our energy efficiency consulting division, helping businesses optimize energy use and reduce costs.
  • 2015: Entered the renewable energy market, delivering cutting-edge solar and wind power solutions.
  • 2018: Developed advanced automation and control systems, enhancing operational efficiency for various industries.
  • 2020: Reached a milestone of completing over 500 projects across Indonesia, solidifying our reputation as a trusted industry leader.

Leadership

Estiawan Mandal

Chief Executive Officer

Estiawan Mandala co-founded ElectroTech Indonesia in 2004 with a vision to revolutionize the electrical services industry in Indonesia. With over 25 years of experience in electrical engineering and project management, Estiawan has been the driving force behind the company's growth and innovation.

“Our mission is to empower Indonesia with innovative electrical solutions that drive progress and sustainability. I am proud of our team's dedication and the impact we've made over the years.”

Gada Prakasa

Chief Technology Officer

Gada Prakasa joined ElectroTech Indonesia in 2008 as Chief Technical Officer, bringing with him extensive expertise in electrical engineering and renewable energy technologies. He is responsible for overseeing all technical operations and ensuring the company's solutions are cutting-edge and reliable.

Kariman Permadi

Head of Renewable Energy

Kariman Permadi joined ElectroTech Indonesia in 2015 to lead the company’s renewable energy division. With a deep understanding of sustainable energy systems, he has been instrumental in positioning ElectroTech as a leader in the green energy sector.

About ElectroTech

Changing the way Indonesia is powered

History

ElectroTech Indonesia was founded in 2004 with a vision to revolutionize the electrical services industry in Indonesia. What began as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a leading provider of comprehensive electrical solutions. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has driven our growth and success over the years.

Our milestones over the years:

  • 2004 : ElectroTech Indonesia was established with a focus on electrical system design and installation.
  • 2008 : Expanded services to include maintenance and troubleshooting, ensuring long-term reliability for our clients' electrical systems.
  • 2012 : Launched our energy efficiency consulting division, helping businesses optimize energy use and reduce costs.
  • 2015 : Entered the renewable energy market, delivering cutting-edge solar and wind power solutions.
  • 2018 : Developed advanced automation and control systems, enhancing operational efficiency for various industries.
  • 2020 : Reached a milestone of completing over 500 projects across Indonesia, solidifying our reputation as a trusted industry leader.
